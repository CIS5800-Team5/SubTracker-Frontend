Our Story

During a meeting, our team realized the amount of subscriptions people have nowadays. One of the problems that consumers face is the amount of subscriptions they have. From this, we decided to create a platform that would help users manage all of their subscriptions.

Our Goal

At SubTracker, we want our users to users feel at ease while fully utilizing SubTracker to track and manage their subscriptions. We want to make the experience simple and easy for users to track and manage their subscriptions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help users control and manage their evergrowing list of subscriptions. At the end of the day, people shouldn't pay for subscriptions they don't use. Consumers should be able to enjoy all of their subscriptions while saving as much as they can.
